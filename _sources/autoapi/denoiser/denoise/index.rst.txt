.. py:function:: mp_pca(volume_sequence, patch_shape, patch_overlap, mask=None, recombination='weighted', threshold_scale=1.0)

   Marshenko-Pastur PCA denoising method

   :param volume_sequence: The volume shape to denoise
   :type volume_sequence: numpy.ndarray
   :param patch_shape: The patch shape
   :type patch_shape: tuple
   :param patch_overlap: the overlap of each pixel
   :type patch_overlap: tuple
   :param mask: A boolean array, defining a ROI in the volume. Only patch with voxels in the ROi will be processed.
   :type mask: numpy.ndarray
   :param recombination: The recombination method of the patch. "weighted" or "mean"
   :type recombination: str
   :param threshold_scale: An extra factor for the patch denoising.
   :type threshold_scale: float

   :returns: numpy.ndarray: The denoised sequence of volume
             numpy.ndarray: The weight of each pixel after the processing.
             numpy.ndarray: If possible, the noise variance distribution in the volume.
   :rtype: tuple

   .. rubric:: Notes

   .. seealso:: :obj:`denoiser.space_time.lowrank.MPPCADenoiser`
